    How to Rent a Room

    Select a room

    We help you get a room for rent
    Browse through our database and select the room that best matches your requirements.We have a lot of rooms based on all areas in towns that we operate in.

    Commission

    We take a Commission of 15%
    After selecting the room that best matches your requirements, we take a commission of 15%. The 15% is calculated multiplying the amount of the room per month with the number of months you are renting for.

    Payment

    You then pay your rent to the Landlord
    At this stage, we take you to go see the room you are renting and introduce you to your new Landlord. After settling with your Landlord, you then move in immediately.
