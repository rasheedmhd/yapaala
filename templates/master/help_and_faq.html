Help and Frequently Asked Questions

How does Yapaala Work?

Yapaala is a platform to connects Property owners to Tenants.

We constantly go round any of the towns in which we operate in, and populate the platform with houses with empty rooms made available for rent by the property owners.

Property owners can also give us a call (0553727115) to come and populate our platform with their properties or reach us at our office in the Garu township adjacent Na-Laku Enterprise

To rent a room, all our users need to do, is to create an account for free and navigate through the houses, which are categorised by area and when they find what they want they can contact us and make a one time payment to us then we reach out to the property owner and reserve the room

Other Services

We are also are working on releasing these services very soon to our cherished users.

Hotel Booking: We are working with various hotels in the district to allow our users to be able to book hotel reservations directly on our platform. Artisans: Our Artisans service provides our users with services of artisans such us painters, masons, room decorators, electricians etc by allowing these artisans to create profiles showing what they do and their contact details. Blogging: We are also creating a blog where we will write and publish meaninful content on how to decorate your room, how to get rental discounts and where to get cheap rents and a lot of other cool stuffs.
